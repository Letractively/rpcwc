God's design for prayer is not simply for our cleansing or comfort, but for the carrying out of His purposes.  Prayer is communication with the living and true God.  God inclines His ear to the prayers of His people.  He invites and commands our prayers.  He attaches His promises to our prayers.  He uses our prayers.  There is power in prayer, not simply for us but for action, not in itself but in the One it addresses.  Prayer engages us as God's instruments.


Seeking God

Read Psalm 115:1-8

  • What does verse 3 tell us about the limitations of God?
  • How is God contrasted with idols?
  • God deserves glory because He is God. But what does the psalmist particularly highlight in v.1 for praise?
Turn your eyes to your Father in heaven. Review with Him some of the things he has just told you about Himself.  Spend time exalting Him for them.  Ask God to lead you as you begin this CHOP ministry.  Are there areas you see now in your life for confession, areas where you rob God of the glory that belongs to Him?
