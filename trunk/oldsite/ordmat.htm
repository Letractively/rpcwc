Personal Plan for Outreach

This bulletin style document is intended to help Christ's disciples be more attuned to and involved in their life-spheres by outreach through prayer, word and deed.  It begins with a personal mission statement and directs in involvement for Christ in the lives of the people-environment in which they have been providentially placed.

  Ephesians Prayer Guide

Ephesians is an epistle that deals with the Church.  The Ephesians Prayer Guide draws 14 sections from the epistle and provides items for prayer under each of the sections.  This bulletin style Guide is useful for leading people into praying along lines of Scripture and directing them in kingdom prayer for church and personal revitalization.
