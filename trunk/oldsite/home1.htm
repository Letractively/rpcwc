"I am the vine, you are the branches; he who abides in Me and I in him,
he bears much fruit, for apart from Me you can do nothing."

John 15:5
 
Purpose

Serving our Lord Jesus Christ as His church to glorify and enjoy Him

 		 Values

Exalting the triune God
Edifying the community of believers
Engaging the culture with the Gospel

Mission

For God's glory and by His grace, serving the greater West Chester, PA area, to impart the life of Christ through living in Christ, learning of Christ and leading to Christ.
Vision

To be a branch growing deeply in the Vine of Life,
filling West Chester, PA with the hope of life.
 
Calling

Finding life in Christ,
Bringing life to others
